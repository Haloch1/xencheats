Exodus R6

Exodus can require more careful setup because of HWID-related support.

Invision Chams

Invision is visual-only, so focus on GPU support and display compatibility.

Fortnite Full

Fortnite Full includes multiple systems, so check display mode and game build first.

  • Prepare your member account and keep your key ready for support.
  • Confirm whether you are using fullscreen, windowed, or borderless mode.
  • Start with basic aim and visual settings before adjusting loot options.

Fortnite Ancient

Ancient is the more configurable Fortnite option with aim, visual, and radar-style features.

  • Record your Windows version and CPU type before opening a ticket.
  • Use a starter profile before sharing or importing configs.
  • Ask support before enabling advanced trigger or radar options.

Xim Spoofer

Spoofer setup depends on device state, motherboard mode, and Windows configuration.

  • Do not run setup until your support ticket confirms the correct route.

  • Follow restart instructions exactly to avoid confusing the support flow.
Accounts

Linked NFA

Account products are handled manually so support can verify delivery details.

  • Open a ticket with your order email and preferred contact method.
  • Review account notes before changing any available information.
  • Do not reshare account access after delivery.
Accounts

Stacked PC Account

Stacked accounts include extra inventory notes and should be reviewed after handoff.

  • Confirm the account notes match what support delivered.
  • Ask questions in the same ticket before making major account changes.
  • Keep screenshots of handoff details for your own records.
